<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a new page, component, or other element?">
    Prompt for it and the builder will create it\!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add AI to my app?">
    Ask the builder for an integration such as "I need users to enter a health goal and have it reviewed by AI"
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which AI model is the best?">
    Anthropic's Claude Sonnet remains our top choice for development and is the only model available currently in Create.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I connect to an external API?">
    [Details here. ](/quick-tips/external-apis)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add databases?">
    Yes. Just prompt for any feature or function that requires data storage and it will be built for you and you can also prompt for additional ones. [More info here. ](/builder/databases)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I host my app on Create? Will it scale to handle many users?">
    Yes, most of our users host their project on Create. Hit Publish in the top right. Your app will scale to handle many users. Reach out about our Business or Enterprise plans if concerned.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add users, sign up, and sign in to my app?">
    You can use our built in [User Accounts](/builder/user-accounts) feature.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What language does Create write code in under the hood?">
    Create generates javascript web apps in React / Next.JS and functions in backend serverless functions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I export the code?">
    Yes. Go to the Code tab \> Download to download the Next.JS project.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I fix this error?">
    Check out our [Guide to Prompting](/prompting) and our [Get Help](/support/help) page
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I collect payments for an app I made?">
    We integrate directly with Stripe to help you collect [payments.](/stripe)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I accidentally deleted something I shouldn't have (!!) Is there any way to get it back?">
    Use the [Version History](/essentials) option to attempt to restore. Email us at [hello@create.xyz](mailto:hello@create.xyz) for further help.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I think I found a bug. Where should I report it?">
    Thank you\! Email us at [hello@create.xyz](mailto:hello@create.xyz). Please include a link to the /build route (the link you see when you are in the builder) for your project.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the difference between free vs. Create Pro?">
    Free is limited to a one time credit allotment and daily generation limits. Create Pro gives you renewing credits per month, private projects, custom domains, and priority AI models. Choose your credit tier based on your needs.
  </Accordion>

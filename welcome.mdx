Create is a new AI creative tool that **lets anyone build with natural language**. You can use it to build **sites and web apps**. Create uses AI to turn your instructions into apps built in code.

### Stay in touch

Follow us on [X/Twitter](https://x.com/create_xyz) for product updates and announcements. Join our [Discord community](https://www.create.xyz/discord) to get help, ask questions, and connect with other creators.

## Change Subscription

1. Log in at [anything.com](https://anything.com)
2. Click on "Subscription & Credits"
3. You can see your current plan and credits
4. Select "Update plan"
5. Click on Manage Subscription from the upper right
6. From here you can:
   - Upgrade your plan
   - Downgrade your plan
   - Cancel your subscription

## Invoices, Receipts, and Billing History

To view your invoices, receipts, and billing history:

1. Log in at [anything.com](https://anything.com)
2. Click on "Subscription & Credits"
3. From here you can access all your billing information

## Testing on your iPhone

Test your mobile app on your iPhone using the Anything iOS App. This provides a native preview experience directly on your device, allowing you to test all device features like camera, location, notifications, and more.

![Test Phone](/images/testphone.png)

### How to load your app on the Anything iOS App

1. **Install the Anything iOS App** - Download the Anything iOS App from the App Store on your iPhone

2. **Load your app** - You can load your app in two ways:
   - **Log in** - Sign in with your Anything account and your projects will sync automatically
   - **Scan the QR Code** - In your Anything project editor, find the QR code displayed in the mobile preview area, then open the Anything iOS App and scan it to load your app instantly

3. **Test in Real-time** - Any changes you make in the Anything builder will automatically update in the Anything iOS App, allowing you to test iterations in real-time

4. **Test Device Features** - Test camera, location, notifications, and other device capabilities directly on your iPhone

<Note>
  Device features like camera, location, and barcode scanning won't work in the web preview. Always test on your iPhone using the Anything iOS App for full functionality.
</Note>

## App Compliance Check

Before submitting your app to the App Store, ensure it meets Apple's guidelines and requirements using our automated compliance tool.

1. **Open Publication Menu** - Click the "Publish" button in your mobile project
2. **Select App Store Review** - Choose "App Store Review" from the publication options

<Note>
  The App Compliance Check analyzes your app against Apple's App Store guidelines and provides detailed feedback on any issues that need to be addressed before submission.
</Note>
